Support Train

Aveda Arts & Science Institute Dallas

Building a culture of love + content

The Big Idea

  • Phase Up Day every ~2 months = schoolwide support day
  • Students follow, like, comment, and repost each other
  • Participation is optional, incentivized with competitions + prizes
  • Target: every student graduates with ~300+ real followers
  • Friendly, positive digital footprint for future careers

How It Works – Phase by Phase

1

Set up a clean, professional IG (guided, optional)

2

Follow classmates; comment “wins” and progress

3

Collab posts/reels; tag each other

4

Repost stories; cross-class shoutouts

5

Portfolio push; book real clients from IG

Each Phase Up Day repeats the support loop so growth compounds.

Incentives & Competitions

  • Most new followers (quality + authenticity)
  • Most supportive (likes/comments given)
  • Best before/after transformation post
  • Most helpful tutorial or tip
  • Prizes: tools, gift cards, feature on school page
  • Fair play: no spam, real accounts only
  • Celebrate wins at Phase Up assemblies

Benefits for Students

  • Real engagement + confidence
  • Brand and portfolio started early
  • Peer creativity = constant inspiration
  • Momentum for clients right after graduation

Benefits for the School

  • Unlimited student-generated content for school socials
  • Higher visibility → stronger inquiries & enrollment
  • Supportive culture = retention + reputation
  • Alumni network that keeps the train going
